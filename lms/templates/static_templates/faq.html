${_("Basic Questions")}

Who can register for AshokaX?

At this stage, AshokaX is an experimental platform and only Ashoka University memembers (students, staff and alumni) can register using their Ashoka University email address. We intend to open Ashokax courses for everyone in the future.

How to register for AshokaX?

Learners have a valid Ashoka University email address can create an ashokax account by filling up the registration form. . After you successfully submit the registration form you will have to verify by clicking the verification link sent to your registered email address.

Do I have to pay for taking a course?

No. All the AshokaX courses are free to audit for everyone. We also intend to provide free honor code certificates to anyone who completes the minimum grade requirements set by the instructors.

Can I access AshoakX courses from my smartphone?

Not Yet. Some of the features of the website may not be supported on the mobile devices. But don't worry! We will soon release mobile Apps for AshokaX!

Will I get a certificate for completing AshokaX courses?

We are planning to provide certificates for successful completion of AshokaX courses. At this stage the AshokaX courses are for Audit only.

How do I audit a course?

To audit a course you can simply enroll for an ongoing course before the enrollment deadline. You will full access to all the course materials for the course and can participate in the course as per your interest and availability.

What courses are being offered? When do they start?

Right now we have only a few flagship and experimental courses on this platform. But the number of courses will grow as we scale our operations in the future. You can see the courses being offered on the Courses page. The start and end dates for these courses are mentioned on the course description pages.
